Understand Your Current Routine#

Before you embark on designing a new morning ritual, take time to introspect. Evaluate your current routine and identify:

  • What works well: Perhaps you drink a glass of water first thing and feel a positive impact from it.
  • What feels off: If you’re hitting the snooze button three times, something might need tweaking.
  • Room for improvement: Could you move your workout from evening to morning?

Countless successful people have morning habits, from meditation to marathon training. Knowing what serves and hinders you helps pave the way for effective changes.
