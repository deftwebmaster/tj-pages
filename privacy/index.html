Privacy Policy

TickJoy’s simple, honest privacy policy: no tracking, no shady stuff, no surprises.
June 24, 2025 · Author

Your privacy matters. A lot.

At TickJoy, we keep things simple:
We don’t want to be tracked — and we don’t track you.

No Analytics#

We don’t use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixels, or any other creepy tracking tools.

Our site is served behind Cloudflare, which may log basic technical data (like IP addresses) for security and performance reasons. That data is handled by Cloudflare — we don’t collect or store it ourselves.

Sponsored Links#

Some pages may include sponsored or affiliate links to products we personally recommend. If you click one of those links, we may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you.

These links will always be clearly labeled. We don’t do hidden ads or misleading promotions.

No Accounts. No Cookies. No BS.#

You won’t find signup forms, login pages, or cookie banners here — because we don’t need them. TickJoy is a public site built for open access and clean content.

Questions?#

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us through whatever contact method we eventually provide (probably a minimalist inbox link that doesn’t suck).

Until then, browse freely — no strings attached.
